HeadVR (https://github.com/autoandshare/HeadVR) is an open source VR video player based on VLC.

Features   Usage

Features:

  • Support touch control, bluetooth controller, head control
  • Head Control may be disabled or locked
  • Nodding to play/pause, turning left/right to seek, ...
  • Go to next/prev video in a folder without taking off headset
  • Support Local and network (SMB, FTP, ...) resources
  • Rich settings (brightness, eye distance, video position ...)
  • Force2D mode to make some 3D video more comfortable. In this mode, same picture will be shown to both eyes.
  • Video Options (sbs/tab, full/half, audio track, subtitle ...)
  • Automatic selection of audio track and subtitle based on language keywords in settings

Note:
  • Gyroscope sensor is necessary for head control
  • 3D/VR file name may include tags like sbs, hsbs, fsbs, ou, tab, 180, 360 to indicate its format.
  • To get good experience, be sure to adjust headset and eye distance setting so that inter-lens distance and video distance on the phone screen are the same as your interpupillary distance.


Usage:

Touch Control:

Play/Pause - Touch Right 2/3 of the screen
Forward/Rewind - Swipe Right/Left
Next/Prev Video - Swipe Up/Down
Video Options - Touch Left 1/3 of the screen
Recenter 360/180 Video - Double Click screen

Bluetooth Controller:

Play/Pause - Center Key
Forward/Rewind - Right/Left Key
Next/Prev Video - Down/Up Key

Head Control:

There are 5 basic head motions: Up, Down, Left, Right, Hold still. Each function is a combination of these motions.

Play/Pause - Down, Up (Nod)
Forward - Right, Hold still, Left (Cancel with Up/Down)
Rewind - Left, Hold still, Right (Cancel with Up/Down)
Next Video - Up, Down, Right, Left
Prev Video - Up, Down, Left, Right
Lock Head Control - Up, Right, Left, Down
Recenter 360/180 Video - Left, Right, Left, Right
Toggle Force2D mode - Right, Left, Right, Left

Note:
Seeking is only enabled for 360/180 video when it is paused.
